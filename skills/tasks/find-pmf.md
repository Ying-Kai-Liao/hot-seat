# Find Product-Market Fit Task

## Objective
Determine if there's a real market for this product and who the ideal customer is.

## PMF Questions
- Who has this problem most acutely?
- How are they solving it today?
- Would they pay for a better solution? How much?
- How would they find out about this product?

## Your Approach
1. Identify a specific customer segment
2. Describe their current pain and workarounds
3. Evaluate if this product solves their problem better
4. Suggest how to validate demand before building
